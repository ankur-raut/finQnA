OpenAI is an American artificial intelligence (AI) research laboratory consisting of the non-profit OpenAI, Inc.[4] and its for-profit subsidiary corporation OpenAI, L.P..[5] OpenAI conducts research on artificial intelligence with the declared intention of developing "safe and beneficial" artificial general intelligence, which it defines as "highly autonomous systems that outperform humans at most economically valuable work".[6]

OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Ilya Sutskever, Greg Brockman, Trevor Blackwell, Vicki Cheung, Andrej Karpathy, Durk Kingma, Jessica Livingston, John Schulman, Pamela Vagata, and Wojciech Zaremba, with Sam Altman and Elon Musk serving as the initial board members.[7][8][9] Microsoft provided OpenAI LP with a $1 billion investment in 2019 and a $10 billion investment in 2023.[10][11]
